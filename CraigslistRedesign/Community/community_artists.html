Local Painter Exhibit

Live Music Night

Join us for an unforgettable evening of live music with local bands at the Riverside Café! From indie rock to acoustic folk, enjoy a variety of performances from talented musicians. The event is free to attend, with food and drinks available for purchase. Perfect for music lovers of all kinds!

Sculpture Artist Showcase

Discover the captivating sculptures of local artist David Ramirez. His works combine traditional and modern techniques, exploring themes of human emotion and form. The exhibit is being held at the Downtown Arts Center, featuring sculptures in various mediums including metal, clay, and stone.

Artist: David Ramirez
